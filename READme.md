# Zenvy Payroll - Dashboard UI/UX Mockups

This document describes the layout and components for the Zenvy Payroll SaaS dashboards.

## 1. Executive Dashboard (Admin/HR View)
**Theme:** Dark Mode with Neon Accents (Slate & Cyan)

### Top Row: KPI Cards
*   **Total Headcount:** Big number (e.g., 1,250) | $\uparrow$ 2% vs last month.
*   **Total Monthly Payroll:** \$430.5k | $\downarrow$ 1% (Optimized).
*   **Active Leaves:** 15 Employees today.
*   **Pending Approvals:** 8 Workflows.

### Middle Row: Main Visuals
*   **Left (60%):** **Payroll Trend (Area Chart)** - 12-month series of total spending.
*   **Right (40%):** **Department Distribution (Donut Chart)** - Headcount by IT, HR, Sales, etc.

### Bottom Row: Low Level Details
*   **Left (50%):** **Recent Activity Feed** - "New Employee Added", "Payroll Finalized".
*   **Right (50%):** **Upcoming Holidays / Events**.

---

## 2. Finance & Analytics Dashboard (DS Intern Focus)
**Theme:** Professional Clean White with Gradient Blues

### Top Row: Salary Metrics
*   **Avg Salary:** \$48k
*   **Salary Range:** \$27.5k - \$82.5k
*   **Total Tax Deducted:** \$85k

### Main Content:
*   **Center Large:** **Salary Distribution (Histogram + Box Plot)** - Showing compensation equity.
*   **Bottom Left:** **Departmental Expenses (Stacked Bar Chart)** - Base vs. Bonus vs. Deductions.
*   **Bottom Right:** **Budget Variance (Gauge Chart)** - Actual Spend vs. Budget Limit.

---

## 3. Operations & Workforce (OT Tracking)
**Theme:** High Contrast (Safety/Warning Colors for OT)

### Top Row: Productivity KPIs
*   **Avg Present Days:** 20.5 / 22
*   **Total OT Hours:** 105 hrs
*   **Highest OT Dept:** IT (45 hrs)

### Main Content:
*   **Heatmap:** Attendance check-ins/outs frequency.
*   **OT Leaderboard (Top 10):** Table with profile photos and hours worked.
*   **Line Chart:** OT Trends matching the monthly cycle.
